Features

Everything you need to track your journey

Comprehensive metrics designed for every type of walker — from casual strollers to marathon trainers.

Step Tracking

Real-time pedometer with intelligent motion detection. Counts every step accurately, whether you're on pavement, trails, or treadmills.

Live counting

Distance & Route

GPS-powered route mapping with precise distance measurement in km or miles. View your path on a live interactive map.

GPS accuracy ±2m

Calorie Burn

Personalized calorie calculations based on your weight, height, age, and walking speed. See exactly how much energy you're expending.

Personalized to you

Heart Rate Monitor

Continuous heart rate tracking with zone alerts. Stay in your optimal training zone and get notified when your BPM goes too high or low.

Zone alerts

Elevation Gain

Track vertical ascent and descent with barometric altimeter data. Perfect for hikers and trail walkers who love a good climb.

Barometric precision
